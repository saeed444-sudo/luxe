About Our Vision

Founded in 2010 by award-winning architect Elena Vasquez, Luxe Interiors has redefined the boundaries of interior architecture. Our philosophy blends form and function with an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship.

With studios in New York, Los Angeles, and Milan, our international team of designers brings diverse perspectives to create spaces that tell your unique story while standing the test of time.

We believe that exceptional design should be accessible to those who appreciate it. Our process is collaborative, transparent, and tailored to each client's aspirations.
